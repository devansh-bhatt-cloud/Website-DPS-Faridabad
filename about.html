How It Started

Our Dream Is Global Learning Transformation

Kawruh was founded by Robert Anderson, a passionate lifelong learner, and Maria Sanchez, a visionary educator. Their shared dream was to create a digital haven for knowledge accessible to all. United by their belief in the transformative power of education, they embarked on a journey to build Kawruh. With unwavering dedication, they assembled a team of experts and launched this innovative platform, creating a global community of eager learners, all connected by the desire to explore, learn, and grow.
